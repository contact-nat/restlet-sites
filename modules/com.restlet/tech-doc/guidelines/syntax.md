## Special characters

Inside some block, you need to escape specials characters with a backslash **'\\'**:
- Symboles : \\ \` \*  \_
- Parentheses : \{ \} \[ \] \( \)
- Ponctuations : \#  \. \!
- Signes : \+ \-
